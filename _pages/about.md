I am a deferred Ph.D. student in Economics at Harvard University (beginning Fall 2020) and a Graduate Student Affiliate at the [Center for History and Economics](https://histecon.fas.harvard.edu/) and the [Institute for Quantiative Social Science](https://www.iq.harvard.edu/). My research focuses on political, experimental, and behavioral economics.
